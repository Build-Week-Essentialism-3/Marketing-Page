What is Essentialism?

Our app, Essentialism, shares it's name with the lifestyle which revolves around the disciplined pursuit of less. Put simply, we want to help you focus on what really matters in your life. Whether that be relationships, art, independance, or anything else that matters to you.

For who?

Essentialism is for the person who wants to declutter. For the mother who forgot her passion, the cubicle worker who can't seem to get his spending straight, or even the college student who wants to refocus on her educational career.

Rob Curran

Why?

Clutter takes on more than just a physical form. Our minds get cluttered with schedules, assignments, jobs, hobbies, finances, and more. Yet with the help of Essentialism, we can add structure to our lives in a meaningful way.

Matthew Henry
